課程名稱：漢語語法 (1002)

授課教師：跨文化研究所李子瑄老師

學習對象：對華語文教學有興趣之大眾

學習目標：

The course aims to provide an introduction to recent work on Chinese syntax.Since the field of syntax is not monolithic, both functional and Minimalist/GB perspectives are introduced to inspire students with critical thinking and discussion on the current state of researches.

課程章節：

Based on the summary of recent work on Chinese grammar made by domestic and foreign scholars, the content of this course will be divided into 16 units:

1. Introduction

2. Word Structure

3. Topic, Subject ,and Object in Mandarin

4. Aspect

5. The Basic Structure of Clauses and Nominal Constituents

6. Auxiliary Verbs and Verb Phrases

7. Adverbs and Adverbial Placement

8. The System of Interrogatives

9. The System of Negation

10. Serial Verb Constructions

11. The ba Construction

12. The bei Construction

13. Dou, distributivity and Universal Quantifiers

14. Existential Sentences

15. Ellipsis Constructions

16. Counting and Classifying Eventualities in Chinese
